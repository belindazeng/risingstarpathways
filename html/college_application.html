College Application

Navigating the world of college applications can be tricky and time consuming - we can help.

Picking Your Colleges

There are so many colleges out there - and it can be really hard to pick the right ones. Based on your preferences, we'll help you find the right list of schools to apply to. We'll make sure to include a list of safety, target and reach schools.

Telling your story

We'll help you build a resume to make you stand out. We'll also help you find the right language to showcase your extracurriculars, leadership ability, and more.

Preparing for the Interview

We'll help you talk about yourself in the alumni interview so you can stand out from the other applicants.

Getting A Recommendation

We'll teach you how to cultivate your relationships and pick the right teachers to get a recommendation letter.

Crafting your Essays

Based on your unique experiences, we'll help you brainstorm, craft, and edit your resumes.
